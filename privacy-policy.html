Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

Phone Condition Checker does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. Introduction

Phone Condition Checker is designed to help users evaluate the condition and health of Android devices, especially when buying or selling second-hand phones.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

3. Device Information

The app may access certain device-related information including:

• Device model and manufacturer
• Android version
• SIM card and network status
• Hardware condition (battery, sensors, etc.)

This information is used solely within the app to provide functionality and is never transmitted externally.

4. Permissions Usage

The app requests only the permissions necessary to perform device checks. These permissions are used strictly for functionality and not for tracking or data collection.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

Phone Condition Checker does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Data Security

Since all processing occurs locally on your device, your data remains private and secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
